Findings

The differences between results of 10 Hz GPS devices for short-distance exercise is small. In other words, the accuracy of 10 Hz GPS devices are good for short-distance exercise. The deviations of result are small. (Med,2011)

The best accuracy between Garmin, Polar and other Running Watches is Garmin. Even though the Garmin has some limitations on it. In bad weather, Garmin has slightly more deviations than other. For taking repeated tracks, there are some deviations on turnings. However, the Garmin holds higher accuracy than other similar (GPS Accuracy of Garmin, Polar, and other Running Watches, 2017)
